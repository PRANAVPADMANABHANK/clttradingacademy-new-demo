About Us

We’re here to redefine how trading education is delivered.

At CLT Trading Academy, we’re not here to teach you what everyone else does — we’re here to redefine how trading education is delivered.
Founded by seasoned hedge fund professionals who’ve played the real game and walked away with wins, CLT is built on a mission to cut the noise and reveal how real trading actually works — the way banks, institutions, and exchanges move the markets.
Our elite mentors bring years of high-stakes trading experience straight from the floors of New York to your screen. We teach only what matters — no generic theories, no unnecessary jargon. Just real-world strategies that work in today’s volatile markets.
Whether you’re new to trading or have faced losses before, CLT gives you the blueprint the pros use — with direct guidance, simplified concepts, and proven frameworks to help you grow from confusion to clarity, from hesitation to high-performance.
CLT is not just an academy. It's a movement. A reset. A return to truth in trading.
